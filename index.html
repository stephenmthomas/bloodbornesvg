Bloodborne SVG

Awhile back I used Figma to create a bunch of Bloodborne Vectors

Most of these are straight from the game, a few I made myself. They are all angular and mostly low-data and extremely small file sizes. But this was my first ever Figma project and my first time doing vector work at all, so... there is room for improvment with many of them.

Do what you want with them... no credit or attribution required.
